Welcome To Brick Breaker!!
How To Play:
Use the paddle to bounce the ball and break through a wall of colorful bricks. 
Move swiftly, strategize your shots, and earn points for each brick shattered. 
Be careful not to lose the ball, though as you will lose the game. Compete for high scores and enjoy the timeless thrill of Brick Breaker! 
Ready to smash some bricks and have a blast?

Controls (Default):
"a" = Move left
"d" = Move right

Controls (Alternative):
"left arrow" = Move left
"right arrow" = Move right
